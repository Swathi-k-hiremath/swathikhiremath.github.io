About Me

I am a student at Arizona State University pursuing a Master of Science in Computer Science. I am passionate about machine learning, deep learning, computer vision, and natural language processing. I have experience working on various LLM-based projects and CV-related projects.

In my previous roles as a Research Assistant and Associate Consultant in AI/ML, I worked on developing and training large language models for various applications. I also worked on a project to apply deep learning various tasks involving images and videos.

I am currently working on RLHF solutions for LLMs. I am excited about the potential of reinforcement learning to improve the performance of LLMs on a variety of tasks.

I am looking for opportunities in the field of deep learning. I am particularly interested in working on projects that combine deep learning with natural language processing. I am also interested in expanding my knowledge of reinforcement learning.

I am a highly motivated and results-oriented individual. I am eager to learn new things and I am always looking for new challenges. I am confident that I can make a significant contribution to any team.
